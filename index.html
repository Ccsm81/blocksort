How to play

Swipe a brick — it glides until something stops it.

A brick can only leave through a gate of its exact colour. The long piece has its very own gate.

Grey stone blocks are in the way — clear them out through their own grey gates at the top & bottom.

Surprises as you climb: ❄️ frozen bricks need a swipe to crack first · 🪨 boulders never move · ❓ mystery bricks hide their colour · the big bar can lie sideways.

Clear the whole board to win. Chain exits for a combo. Fewer moves = more ⭐. Stuck? Tap 💡 Hint.
